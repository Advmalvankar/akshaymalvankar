About Me

Enrolled in 2025 with the Maharashtra & Goa State Bar Council, I practice before subordinate courts with a focus on litigation, contract law, and data protection. My experience spans divorce matters, cheque dishonor cases, RTI drafting, and legal notices, reflecting a disciplined approach to drafting and statutory compliance.

  • I hold a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B) and a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com, Accounting and Finance) from the University of Mumbai.
  • I am currently pursuing an LL.M. in Business Law, aiming to integrate corporate advisory and litigation with an emphasis on contract drafting, compliance, and arbitration.
